To increase the chance of placing high-priority labels on the map, you can provide an array of Property.TEXT_ANCHOR locations: the renderer will attempt to place the label at each location, in order, before moving onto the next label. Use `text-justify: auto` to choose justification based on anchor position. To apply an offset, use the textRadialOffset or the two-dimensional textOffset.
